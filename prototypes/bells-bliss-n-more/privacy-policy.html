Information We Collect

When you browse our catalog, book rentals or planning services, or create an account, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, event address, and payment information (processed by our hosted payment provider — we never store full card numbers).

How We Use It

We use this information to fulfill bookings, coordinate delivery and event-day logistics, send order and appointment confirmations, and — only with your consent — send occasional availability and sale updates.

How We Protect It

We limit access to customer information to staff who need it to fulfill your booking, and rely on our payment processor's secure, hosted checkout so we never handle raw card data ourselves.

Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time, and can request a copy or deletion of your account information by contacting us below.
